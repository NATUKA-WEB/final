TRANSPORT

KTM Komuter
KTM Komuter is a commuter train service in Malaysia operated by Keretapi Tinah Melayu (KTMB). It was introduced in 1995 to provide local rain service in Kula Lumpur and the surrounding Klang Valley suburban areas .The train used are air-conditioned electric multirole units. “Park and Ride” facilities are provided at stations at nominal charge.

Go KL City Buss
Its’s a smarter and save money way to travel whole KL. Go-KL city buss is a free service for visitors and residents as well. comfortable air-conditioned, pink coloured single-deck buses now cruise two circular routes covering many of the main sight-seeing attractions in the heart of the city .

LRT
Taking a ride on LRT or light rail transit train is easy. When you are in the station, read the rote map to locate where you are and decide where to go. purchase the ticket or the train token. You can use “touch and go” if you prefer.
