JOHOR

Discover The Jewel Of The South

Johor is Malaysia’s most populous state and a growing economic power player. Most travelers literally skip it but those who stray into the southern gateway to Malaysia will be rewarded with the blissful islands and wild jungles.

Why shold you visit Johor?

There can be a lot of questions why Johor? Why should you visit the city? What make the city special or what to see once you are there? How Johor can be listed among other Malaysia’s most visited cities? Actually, it’s not. Especially if not long ago the town was totally empty and was even out called as a jungle, till its nature wasn’t discovered by visitors.

Discover tropical wild nature!

Like all Malaysia Johor have a tropical and wild nature, what makes tourists to visit the town. Maybe city can’t be proud wit kts big travel history but it can certainly be proud of its amazing and very old architecture dating back from 1600.

If almost all Malaysian cities have new. May say reconstructed and rebuilding history, it doesn’t apply to Johor. It has its own history, culture, deeply clear Asian line mixed with an Eastern culture. An amazing tropical jungle, beautiful wild nature, an amazing beach, botanic garden and historical heritages that are part of the places one shall see here.

Buildings are telling the stories

An old Chinese temple is the oldest temple in Johor built during 19th century. The temple unites five Chinese dialects each of whom favor a different deity with its own statue in the temple.

GLASS TEMPLE

Arulmigu Sri Rajakaliamman glass temple, is major Hindi temple. The temple was built in 1922 and is decorated with glass from inside and outside. This is first and only temple in the world made by glass and is especially beautiful in the sunny weather, when all temple is shining and sparkling with sun rays. The temple has café that serves vegetarian meals for special events
